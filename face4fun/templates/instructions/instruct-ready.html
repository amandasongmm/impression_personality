Instructions

You will see around 100 pairs of faces.

Try to make sense of what most people think and we will compare your response with others.

You may see the same pair multiple times (the left-right order might be flipped). Watch carefully and make consistent choices.

Your HIT will be accepted if more than 80% of your choices align with the majority vote and more than 70% of your own choices are consistent when seeing the same pair.

Press A to choose the left face, press B to choose the right face.

Don't press until both faces are loaded.

Have fun :)
